Flying to Mars

About the project

In the era of active space exploration, flights to the moon became available to rich people from developed countries. Several hundred years have passed since then, during which there has been a new wave of technological development. Now flights to the moon do not surprise anyone.

If you want to experience new sensations, go beyond and bring something radically new into your life, you have every chance to become one of the first lucky ones to conquer the depths of space. For the first time in the history of mankind, we are making flights available toMars.

Until now, only scientists and researchers have conquered the Red Planet. It took hundreds of years and more than one technical revolution to make Mars suitable for colonial life and private visits. The Galaxy project was the first to receive the unique rights to organize private flights to Mars.

Welcome to the new world.
